Type Checker Notes and Tips
✧ Your pokemon must be at Level 100.
✧ Your pokemon must have +spe. Better if +atk +spe / +def +spe. Best if +atk +def +spe.
✧ If you will use a Negative Class, you must do the opposite in terms of battle (if the battle indicates 'normal' then do 'inverse' and vice versa)
✧ If you see an attack that recommends 'V-Create', you can also use the other 4 highest Fire attack power (Mind Blown, Eruption, Blast Burn, Shell Trap). Same goes with 'Freeze Shock', you can also use 'Ice Burn' etc..
✧ If you see a pokemon with multiple x2 or x4 weaknesses other than the indicated attack, you can always use the other types. For example an Onix. I put Water Spout (150) but you can also use Frenzy Plant (150), Focus Punch (150), etc. As long as it has the same attack power of the indicated attack in the output, it will deliver same result.
✧ All Information is based on Heodenings_Waltz's Guide: Grinding Exp and Coins using VIP Elite Four Trainers.
How to use Type Checker Trainers?
✧ Select one or two types from the list of Pokémon Types.
✧ Clickbutton to generate all possible trainers based on the selected types.
✧ The results will show Attack Moves and Notes, providing information about the opponent trainers.
✧ Star () represents the ineffective/immune attacks of your opponent's against your pokemon.
*For Example, Your pokemon Mawile (Steel and Fairy) against VIP Sidney. Since there are 5 stars, All the attack(s) may hit your Mawile will be ineffective. Making it the best candidate for the selected types and trainers.
Note: 5 stars is the highest and 1 star is the normal rating
✧ The switch on/off checkbox beside the Battle Mode button can be use to change the Battle Mode of each trainers (Normal to Inverse or vice versa).
✧ The Move's background color is based on the color of Pokémon Types. If the move is Overheat that means it's a Fire-Type Move.
